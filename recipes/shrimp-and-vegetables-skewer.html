Shrimp and Vegetables Skewer Recipe

Easy to Make Shrimp and Vegetable Skewers

When you're in need of a simple and healthy shrimp recipe, look no further than these easy to make shrimp and vegetable skewers. All the ingredients for this delicious recipe can be found at most local grocery stores. From start to finish, this cook should take no longer than 35 minutes before you're ready to enjoy the meal.

Wild caught large shrimp doused in lemon and skewered alongside broccoli, onions, and red and green bell peppers. Sprinkle the skewers with freshly grated parmesean cheese, and begin the feast!

Ingredients

  • 8oz of Wild Caught Large Shrimp
  • 4 small pieces of broccoli per skewer
  • 1 small onion cut into 1x1 inch squares
  • 1 small green bell pepper cut into 1x1 inch squares
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • Desired amount of parmesean cheese
  • 1 1/2 large lemons
  • 1/2 tbsp of salt
  • 1/2 tbsp of pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp of thyme
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

Steps

We have separated the steps below into two sections for ease of reading: shrimp and vegetables. It should be noted that these sections should be read through and completed simultaneosly in order to ensure quick preparation time.

Shrimp

  1. Slice 1 lemon in half and squeeze juice into a plastic bag or glass container.
  2. Place 8oz of wild caught shrimp into the lemon juice, and let marianate for 15-20 minutes
  3. Season the shrimp with salt, pepper, thyme, gralic powder, and paprika in the amounts described on the ingredients list.
  4. Place a medium skillet on the stove under medium heat, and add 1 tbsp of butter.
  5. Once the butter is melted, place shrimp on the skillet and pan sear for 4-6 minutes. Make sure to flip the shrimp halfway through the cook time to ensure they are evenly cooked.
  6. Once the shrimp have finished cooking, remove them from pan and sandwich the shrimp between a set of vegetables (broccoli, red/green bell peppers, and onions) on the skewer.
  7. Repeat the last step until the skewer is filled from end to end.

Vegetables

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut small onion, red bell peppers, and green bell peppers into 1x1 inch squares
  3. Place broccoli, red/green bell peppers, and onions onto a baking sheet.
  4. Douse the vegetables on the baking sheet in 1 tbsp of olive oil and make sure to spread evenly.
  5. Squeeze 1/2 of a lemon onto the vegetables on the baking sheet.
  6. Lightly season the vegetables with salt, pepper, and garlic powder
  7. Once preheated to 425 degrees Fahrenheit, place the baking sheet in the oven for 12 minutes (15 minutes of more crisp is desired).
  8. Remove baking sheet from oven, and sandwich each set of vegetables (broccoli, reg/green bell pepper, and onions) between two shrimp.
  9. Repeat the last step until the skewer is filled from end to end.

Relax and enjoy your meal!
