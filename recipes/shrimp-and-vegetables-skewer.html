Shrimp and Vegetables Skewer Recipe

Easy to Make Shrimp and Vegetable Skewers

When you're in need of a simple and healthy shrimp recipe, look no further than these easy to make shrimp and vegetable skewers. All the ingredients for this delicious recipe can be found at most local grocery stores. From start to finish, this cook should take no longer than 35 minutes before you're ready to enjoy the meal.

Wild caught large shrimp doused in lemon and skewered alongside broccoli, onions, and red and green bell peppers. Sprinkle the skewers with freshly grated parmesean cheese, and begin the feast!
